P1.1: Idea

Amore

This is a fictional business that sells love. They sell various potions like love potions, potions for revenge against loved ones who wronged you, potions for feeling like you are loved or in love, and many more. They sell other items like glue for broken hearts, heart repair kits, platonic feelings, lockets (photos not included), charms, and other magical items. The business has strict warnings on their page mentioning that they are not responsible for the outcome of some of their items with prolonged use.

Angel Archives

Angels Archives is a company that creates and sells jewelry based off of famous works of art. They sell all kinds of jewelry with various metals, colors, and sizes. Customers can select different jewels and colors and develop their own artistic jewelry piece if they so desire, or they can choose from one of the premade designs.

Apothecary Estelle

Estelle is a local apothecary who has all of your medicinal needs. Every remedy is hand prepared and shipped with care, and results are guaranteed almost instantly. Remedies exist for everything, and Estelle means everything, so if you are plagued with an ailment and need it cured book a consultation now!
