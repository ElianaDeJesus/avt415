Amore: User Scenario Mapping

Actor - Lena Martinez

Motivator - Recent heartbreak

Intention - Revenge on her ex

Action - Lena goes onto the site and chooses the forbidden magical items category. She selects her desired potion and adds it to her cart. She then signs the waiver that comes up at the checkout screen in order to make sure Amore is not liable for any damages or unexpected outcomes, and she proceeds to place the order.

Resolution - Lena gets her item and uses it on her ex then leaves a positive review of Amore on the site.

Scenario

Lena Martinez is a heartbroken girl who just got cheated on by her ex boyfriend. She wanted revenge, so she called her best friend. Her best friend remembered a shop she ordered from that deals in magical items. Lena then went onto the website and searches for the "forbidden" magical items. She signs the waiver and orders a revenge of the heart potion to use on her ex. It is scheduled to arrive in 5 days after Lena gets through the place order screen. It worked so well that Lena went onto the Amore website and left a five star review and ordered a heart repair kit for her broken heart.
